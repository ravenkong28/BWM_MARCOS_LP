Decision Support System BWM-MARCOS Method

The Best Worst Method, or BWM, was introduced by Dr. Jafar Rezaei in 2015 as a method to address limitations in existing comparison methods. Commonly used Multi-Criteria Decision Making methods like AHP often result in inconsistent paired comparison matrices.

Measurement of Alternatives and Ranking according to Compromise Solution, abbreviated as MARCOS, is a method used to evaluate a set of alternatives in relation to a set of decision criteria. This method is based on defining the relationship between alternatives and reference values (ideal and anti-ideal alternatives). Based on this relationship, the corresponding utility function for the considered alternatives is calculated, and compromise rankings are formulated in relation to the ideal utility function.
